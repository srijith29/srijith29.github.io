My thoughts on meditation before reading the chapter

Since I had a bit of experience in meditation and mindfulness with various traditions, I had a good understanding of meditation. Meditation as per my understanding is a very personal affair. You can try and learn methods or follow instructions but the one that works might be the one you developed over a period of time and might contain inputs from various sources or none.

New learnings

The concept of meta-mindfulness was intersting as well as the analogy with learning to cycle. Another learning I had from the chapter is the importance of forgiving oneself when you loose your concentration.

Meditation technique

One technique that stands out is the method of delaying the response by analysing the cause and being mindful of the response if we choose to respond.

Musings

Meditation is a journey where one discovers ones own path
