What did you learn from minfulness conversation?

Mindfulness conversation consists of 3 steps. 1. Mindful listening, 2. Looping and 3. Dipping. Mindful listening is giving complete undivided attention to the speaker. Looping is the process by which we repeat what the speaker had conveyed to their satisfaction and to repeat it until we get it right. Dipping is listening to our own internal distraction while performing the other processes and acknowledging it. I will be using dipping a lot as it can be used in any formal or informal setting even without the participation of the other person
