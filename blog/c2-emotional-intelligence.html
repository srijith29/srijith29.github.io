Emotional Intelligence

The chapter gives the importance of EQ and its benefits. People with higher EQ has higher chances of success in life and at work. Stellar performance, Outstanding leadership capabilities etc are some benefits of better EI. Emotional Intelligence can be structured into a combination of 1.Self Awareness 2. Self Regulation 3. Motivation 4. Empathy 5. Social Skills. The important aspect of the emotional intelligence is that it's learned. Hence constant practice can improve the EI of a person. Happiness, Kindness, Optimism, and Resilience could be some of the side effects of a better EI.

Training EI

EI can be increased by training and increasing the attention is a crucial part of it. EI depends how calm the mind is and increased attention and by increasing the time between stimulus and response can positively affect EI. By practising mindfullness, the attention quality increases which inturn increases the EI.

Steps to increase EI

  • First increase attention and time between response and stimulus using mindfulness.
  • Self regulation- Once in mindfulness regulate the responses by labelling the emotions with words
  • Once attention is made strong use it to monitor the body responses to stimulus
  • Body responses can be indicators of trigger and responses can be managed as per the situation
  • Strengthen intuitions,this can inturn strengthen perceptions

Mindfulness in 2 minutes

Sit for 2 minutes watching breath or just sit without any agenda. This is a simple mindful practice. If practiced often this can bring mental clarity and calmness. Watching breath or just sitting can also be interchanged if required. This practise is an effective way to monitor the mind. One may not get to perfect it at the beginning. Its hard to not to focus on thoughts and just being there. But as they say practise makes perfect. 2 minutes seems daunting in the beginning.
